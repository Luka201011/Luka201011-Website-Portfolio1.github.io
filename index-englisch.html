About me

Hey! I'm Luka Jevremovic, born in 2010. I'm fascinated by computers, technology, natural sciences, programming, and mathematics, and that's why I see my future in computer science. I chose the career computer scientist, because I enjoy developing my own applications and working as part of a team to create and implement new concepts. With this website, I want to show what I've learned and share my enthusiasm for computer science. I'm honest, social, organized, focused, and enjoy collaborating with others.
